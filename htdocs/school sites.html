School Sites






School Sites Page has a list of sites and Google Chrome extensions that can help you with studying. Do you like chilling? Need assistance with your assignment? Want some comforting sites that can help you vent your negativity? School Sites have those sites you are looking for.

Google Chrome Extensions















coffeelings

coffeelings is an extension that records and tracks your mood daily using the shades of brown representing the different flavors and types of coffee.












Dualless

Dualless is an easy-to-use Chrome extension that makes splitting your screen into windows way~ easier. It will definitely aid you with multitasking or writing papers while watching a video.












Google Dictionary

Google Dictionary is a browser extension where you just have to double click a word at any site, and the browser will immediately show you its definition.












Momentum

Momentum is a browser extension that makes you your personal dashboard to help you focus, stay organized, and be motivated to continue with your tasks. It features todo lists, photos, motivational quotes, and weather.












Todoist

Todoist is a minimal to-do list maker. It lists and organizes all your undone, pending, and done tasks in one place.












Weava

Weava is an extension that will help you highlight key points in websites and PDFs. Just select a word, phrase, or sentence and it will instantly be highlighted. It will also organize your research and essays.

Academic Sites















Desmos

Desmos is an advanced graphing calculator where you can make graphs and input equations and expressions. It is accessible both as a website and mobile application.












GeoGebra

GeoGebra is an application and website for interactive geometry, algebra, statistics, and calculus learning. It can be an algebra and graphing calculator.












Physics Classroom

Physics Classroom is an interactive sites with images, lectures, and exercises for learning PHYSICS.












WolframAlpha

WolframAlpha is a free website for accessing different educational and supplementary materials for a variety of subjects/courses. It also computes and searches for answers once you type a question, problem, or expression.

Chilling Sites















brain.fm

brain.fm is a site with a variety of modes whether you want to relax, focus, meditate, nap, or sleep — there is always a unique mode that the site will provide. You can also customize your settings like setting the timer, adjusting the volume, and choosing a mood that you want to enter (which also has its own set of songs and genre).












I Miss My Cafe

I Miss My Cafe is a site with multiple audios like barista, coffee cups, other customers, sunny day, rainy day, fireplace, etc. to make you actually feel like you are in a cafe — and you can also adjust the volumes. Additionally, there is a provided playlist that you can play as you study or just chill in general.












lifeat.io

lifeat.io has multiple spaces with different moods that you can choose from. Art, programming, cafe, k-pop, library, nature, lo-fi, etc. — there are numerous dashboards that you can choose from! You can also set a timer for a short break, long break, or a studying timer. Each dashboard also has its own playlist that you can play while chilling.












studywithme.io

. studywithme.io is a minimal space where you fan set a timer in general, and timers for long breaks and short breaks. Like the dashboards in lifeat.io, this site has its own playlist you can chill to.

Comforting Sites















Pixel Thoughts

Pixel Thoughts is a comforting website where you can input your current worry. This worry will then be put in a pixel, and it will soon fade away into nothing. This site is effective for you if you have something you worry about at the moment.












This Site Will Self-Destruct

This Site Will Self-Destruct is a site that you can vent to. All your vents or rants will be there and as soon as 24 hours pass, the site will delete all the data you have inputted.
