School Tips

School Tips Page contains estudyanTIPS and focuSSISTANT, both of which are a list of tips and pieces of advice that may help a PISAY scholar in her studies. While estudyanTIPS concentrates on assisting the isko/iska with studying strategies, focuSSISTANT will aid you with improving your time management, organization, and focus.

School Apps

School Apps Page finds downloadable and easy-to-access apps for our scholars. May it be for focusing, organizing your files, or expanding your knowledge, school apps will list down apps that can help you.

School Sites

School Sites Page has a list of sites and Google Chrome extensions that can help you with studying. Do you like chilling? Need assistance with your assignment? Want some comforting sites that can help you vent your negativity? School Sites have those sites you are looking for.
