School Apps






School Apps Page finds downloadable and easy-to-access apps for our scholars. May it be for focusing, organizing your files, or expanding your knowledge, school apps will list down apps that can help you.

Apps for Capturing Documents















CamScanner

CamScanner is a mobile application that will scan your photo and turn it into a PDF, JPG, PNG, DOCX, etc. You can also add a filter and adjust the settings of the photo you took.












Microsoft Office Lens

Microsoft Lens has similar use to Camscanner. You can scan your photo and turn it into another file format and crop it.

Apps for Creating















Canva

Canva is an app for designing where you can access a multitude of templates, design elements, upload multiple files (audio, image, or video), and more! You can create slide presentations, posters, videos, brochures, cards, logos, etc.












Grammarly

Grammarly is a typing assistant that checks for spelling, grammar, punctuation, clarity, engagement, and delivery errors. Artificial intelligence is used to recognize the errors and also finds and looks for a suitable replacement.

Apps for Focusing















Forest

Forest is a productivity app which enables users to manage their time in an engaging and enjoyable manner and overcome their smartphone addiction. Using forest can aid you with concentrating on your activities and take your attention away from distractions.












Pomodoro Timer

Pomodoro Timer is a customizable application and website with an aesthetic design that can set a timer and list down your tasks.

Apps for Journaling















DailyBeans: Simpler Journal

DailyBeans: Simpler Journal — this app will record and summarize your day just by tapping a few buttons. You can add your mood there, as well as the activities you did for the day












Daylio

Similar to DailyBeans, Daylio Journal is also an app that will record your day by tapping a button from the choices given to you. Your hobbies, workouts, moods, etc. — they are all summarized in a note.

Apps for Organizing















GitMind

GitMind is a downloadable application for organizing your thoughts, lecture notes, and notes into a mind map. The app has customizable templates that you can edit,an aesthetic design, and features that will help you organize.












Notion

Notion is highly efficient as it is an application that you can use for customizing your own dashboard. You can add pages, tables, toggles, headers, media, embed links, etc. — you can use it for customizing for fun, organizing your files, noting and jotting down to-dos, and more!

Apps for Reviewing/Studying















Quizlet

Quizlet is an app that makes use of flashcards for reviewing purposes. It is an efficient app for reviewing for tests and exams, especially if you aim to study definitions.












StudySmarter

StudySmarter is a website and application for creating notes that will assist you with studying and reviewing for your upcoming quizzes, exams, and tests.
